Sonic the Hedgehog 4: Episode II Framerate Fix

Methods (even though there is currently only one at the moment) to fix the framerate issue in Sonic the Hedgehog 4: Episode II on Windows.

Windows 11

To fix the framerate issues in Sonic the Hedgehog 4: Episode II on Windows 11, you will need to change the refresh rate to 60 Hz. To do this, open the Settings, then navigate to System > Display > Advanced display. After that, go to Choose a refresh rate and change it to 60 Hz.
